My journey

I'm Om Bharti, an embedded systems engineer driven by the challenge of making hardware intelligent. My curiosity started with a simple radio kit and exploded into a passion for signal processing and semiconductor physics. I've spent the last two years building real-time DSP engines on 8-bit microcontrollers, designing LoRa-based communication systems that work where cellular fails, and simulating next-generation TFET biosensors.

I'm now seeking internship and full-time opportunities where I can apply my expertise in embedded firmware, wireless systems, and semiconductor device engineering to build products that matter. I believe in clean code, cycle-accurate optimization, and systems that just work.
